Project Activities

Our project endeavors to address the urgent issue of marine plastic pollution by raising awareness and fostering meaningful engagement. Through a multifaceted approach that incorporates education, collaboration, and creative expression, aiming to inspire action and drive positive change within the community.

We are highly motivated by the effective educational approach of “Active Learning” through our activities with youths. Traditional learning methodologies have been proven to not function well and have less impact for youths in the environmental curriculums. Thus, practicing and approaching “Active Learning” in our activities would enhance the better understanding of youths.

Activity 01

Activity 01 leverages social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, etc.) to raise awareness about marine plastic pollution. By sharing educational yet easy-to-understand content, we aim to engage youth, highlight the urgency of the issue, and inspire action. Our goal is to spark interest, spread awareness, and encourage positive change through these platforms.

Activity 02

The Clean Seas: Creative Minds Poster Challenge aimed to engage youth and raise awareness about marine plastic pollution. A select of students from Hinthar International School showed interest in workshops that covered the environmental impact of plastic pollution and ways to communicate this issue through art.

The workshops provided students with knowledge about the issue of plastic pollution and taught them effective visual communication techniques. The students then applied these insights to create their own posters, using creativity to express the importance of addressing the issue. This initiative empowered youth to actively contribute to environmental advocacy through their artwork.
